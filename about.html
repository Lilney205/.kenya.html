About Kenya

Kenya is a country in East Africa with a rich history, diverse cultures, and stunning natural beauty. It is known for its savannahs, the Great Rift Valley, and abundant wildlife, including lions, elephants, and rhinos.

Wildlife

Kenya is renowned for its wildlife and is home to some of the most famous national parks and game reserves in the world, including the Maasai Mara, Amboseli, and Tsavo. Visitors can experience the "Big Five" (lion, elephant, buffalo, leopard, and rhinoceros) and witness the spectacular annual migration of wildebeest and zebras in the Maasai Mara.

Tourism

Tourism is a vital part of Kenya's economy. The country offers a variety of attractions, from pristine beaches along the Indian Ocean to the snow-capped peaks of Mount Kenya. Adventure seekers can enjoy activities such as safaris, hiking, and snorkeling, while cultural enthusiasts can explore traditional Maasai villages and historical sites like Fort Jesus in Mombasa.
